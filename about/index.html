关于博客

Welcome to my digital sanctuary—a space where thoughts converge, experiments unfold, and creativity takes shape. Compared to the immediacy and fragmentation of social media, this space focuses more on depth and the purity of expression.

On platforms like Moments or QQ Space, content is often subjected to restrictions and censorship, which undoubtedly compresses the freedom of expression. Blogs, on the other hand, offer creators a greater sense of autonomy. Here, there are no complex rules, nor are there gray exclamation marks signaling content that might disappear at any moment. “The fate of expression does not lie in being understood.” Some words simply need to be said, even if no one is listening.

Moreover, blogs have a unique charm in their medium. Moments and QQ Space rely on a passive, unsolicited stream of information. Blogs, however, are different—they require active visits and a deliberate pause to listen attentively. This “sense of ritual” imbues them with more weight, making the act of expression feel more earnest and free.

As for the content of this site, I plan to write about things that interest me, including but not limited to technical learning notes, photography projects, personal insights, and casual musings from daily life. The content here can be seen as a superset of the updates I might post on Moments or QQ Space (of course, whether it’s this blog, QQ Space, Moments, or even my photo album, they are all subsets of my life). I hope these entries won’t just be cold collections of information but can also provide some inspiration to you, the reader.

PS: As I feel I am currently in a period of intense learning and still lack the ability to produce high-quality content, updates to this blog may not be very frequent.
